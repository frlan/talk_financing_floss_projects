About me

Why

You have costs and don't want to/can't pay everything out of your own wallet

Infrastructure

You have to have some tools you
and your users can work with.

  • Webpage: Blog, forum, webpages itself (hosting), domains(!), ...
  • Communication services: IRC, mailing lists, ...
  • Collaboration services: Source hosting, issue/feature/bug/task tracker, decision making
  • At some point: Backoffice

Teamwork

A project consists of more than 1 guy working alone in some cellar

  • Meetings
  • Conferences
  • Hackatons, sprints, summits

Public Relations

Do cool shit and talk about!

  • Booths at events
  • Stickers and other merchandising
  • Flyer, posters, ...
  • Sponsoring

Opportunity costs

You need some food and some place to stay

When doing a FLOSS project, you might not earn money for your living

Remember: If a FLOSS project grows, team's efforts will also need to grow

How

Donations

  • Users like to send little pieces of money
  • Any kind of micropayment: PayPal, flattr, bitcoin, ...
  • A few will send bigger amounts (>500kc)
  • Keep costs for payment services in mind

Services around your project

  • Offer services around your project, customers want to pay money for
  • Examples:
    • Premium support
    • Schoolings (and certificates!)
    • Customizations

Merchandising

  • Clothes, stickers, ... with your logo
  • CD/USB/* with your stuff on it
  • Events around your project

Get hired for what you are doing

  • Many companies hire experts on projects they are using
  • Some companies are willing to support development with money
  • Take part on projects like summer of code or local supporting programs your city/country/* might offer

Get some kickstart

  • Many plattforms and people offering "kickstart" or venture capital
  • Build up a plan which goal you like to achieve
  • Think what makes your project interesting for them
  • Take care as other might take control over your project

Don't ...

  • Don't outsell your project
  • No adverts on your webpage
  • No selling of user data
  • No sponsored links or referals program
  • Don't include 3rd party stuff just because of money

Issues!

Legal

  • Earning money might causes need for taxes and other fees
  • Be prepared!
  • Setting up some kind of company, foundation might solve issues even for small project (overhead!)

Friendship

  • Somebody needs to have control about money
  • Keep care this one is having the right spirit
  • Arguments about money can destroy an project

Live support

You probably won't be able to pay you rent with a FLOSS project

IF you want somebody to pay money for your service, send them an invoice

The End
